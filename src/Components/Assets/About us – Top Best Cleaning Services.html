About us

Top Best Cleaning Services Ltd. is an established company with a legal entity and impressive portfolio clients. Registered in 2007 with Registrar of Companies – Uganda, it has evolved in a period of 10 years in terms of service and management to serve our clients better day by day and Year after Year, in this period as Top Best Cleaning Services, we have  trained our staff and acquired state of art machinery for Cleaning, fumigation, Waste handling, etc  to increase timely delivery and  efficiency of services to our clients.

VISION

To be champions & masters of a clean and pest free environment in the region where we operate

MISSION

To provide excellent quality services and offer competitive prices that are customer friendly all year round.

VALUES

  • Honesty & integrity
  • Confidentiality
  • Effectiveness
  • Timely delivery
  • Quality work

COMPANY COLOURS

The company has two official colors (blue, Green and yellow).  The customer is free to make a choice of the color suitable for the site.

STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES OF THE COMPANY

  • To ensure quality service and customer satisfaction year in year out.
  • To ensure a clean and pest/rodent free environment.

  • To invest in long term strategies by making correct and informed decisions for quick growth and fulfillment of stakeholders expectations.
  • To continuously uplift the standards of our staff by continuous on job training
  • To aim at exceeding our customers expectations
  • To focus on the use to environmentally friendly chemicals, detergents, machinery, equipments and tools.
  • To adhere to safety measures during execution of our duties
  • To offer consistent competitive prices to our customers.
  • To be number one employer in terms of remuneration and conditions of work
